The VAGA X process

The process consist of getting best quality product from guarantee producers to YOU, the final consumer.
Providing from Producers or Manufacturer like PAPYE Brand is the first step in the process, and we make sure that we store the products in the proper place. Our warehousing process ensures that products are reakily available when needed for distribution.
Transportation is part of the process too, we make sure that goods are transported from our warehouses to different points of distribution.
VAGA X is also open to collaborate with wholesalers and Retailers, you can buy products in large and small quantities so you can sell directly to consumers. Either your store is physical, online or both
The final consumers, this is where the VAGA X process is very sensitive. We make sure that the end user who purchased and use the products has the highest satisfaction.
We cover the two distribution channels, either Direct Distribution or Indirect Distribution. While we preserve the key functions which are: Inventory Management, Order Processing, Logistics and Transportation, Customer Service and Return handling.
