What is PAPYE?

PAPYE™ is a sanitary paper-based company which is specalize in the production, distribution and innovation of eco-friendly and hygienic paper products for personal and commercial use.

PAPYE is a trade mark concept implemented by Black Haitian in the United States of America, all of the PAPYE products are proudly wrapped and assembled by Haitian Folks living in the USA"
